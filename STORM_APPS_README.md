# Storm Apps - Ultra-Premium Luxury Website

## 🌟 Overview

An exclusive, high-end website for Storm Apps that positions the brand as an elite digital partner. This is not a typical tech website—it's a luxury brand experience in the world of technology.

## ✨ Design Philosophy

**Luxury Minimalism + Futuristic Precision + Arabic Elegance**

- Emotional tone: Prestige, power, exclusivity, intelligence
- Think: "If a luxury brand built a tech company"
- Every detail crafted to feel rare, expensive, and unforgettable

## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette

**Dark Mode (Default)**
- Background: Deep black (#0A0A0A)
- Foreground: Warm white (#FAFAF8)
- Accent: Metallic gold (#D4AF37)
- Highlights: Controlled neon blue (#00D9FF) and violet (#A855F7)

**Light Mode**
- Background: Soft ivory (#FAFAF8)
- Foreground: Rich black (#0A0A0A)
- Maintains luxury aesthetic with warm, premium tones

### Typography

**Arabic (RTL-first)**
- Primary: Tajawal (modern, clean, elegant)
- Secondary: Cairo (geometric, modern)

**English**
- Primary: Space Grotesk (modern sans-serif)
- Secondary: Syne (geometric, refined)
- Accent: Orbitron (futuristic)

## 🎭 Interactions & Animations

### Animation Principles
- Slow, confident, cinematic
- No fast or cheap animations
- Smooth transitions (0.3s - 0.8s duration)
- Spring physics for natural feel
- Glow and blur effects for luxury

### Hover States
- Scale transforms (1.02 - 1.1)
- Color transitions to gold accent
- Glow effects appear
- Icons rotate 360°
- Shimmer effects

### Scroll Reveals
- Fade in + slide up
- Staggered delays for lists
- Opacity + transform transitions
- Once-only animations (no repeat)
